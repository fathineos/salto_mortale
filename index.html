The team

The Salto Mortale Puppet Theatre was founded in Athens. Their members have different backgrounds and work on puppetry as a way to express themselves. They are involved in all stages of the production, from the conceptualisation to the materialisation. They like to travel and perform on the street, theaters and bars as well as interact with artists from different cultural backgrounds. They discovered the world of puppet theatre in AYUSAYA Puppet Theatre (Stathis Markopoulos) and they are members of the Greek branch of UNIMA. Awards 1st New Puppeteers award in 16th Puppet-Mime International Festival of Kilkis
